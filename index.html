Read our story

We've been Making The Delicious Foods Since 1999

I love how dinner time brings people together, which is why at Bill's I wanted to create a proper night-out experience; a real home-from-home atmosphere; a healthy and indulgent menu and wine list, with candles and lots of little touches to give the place a real glow. .

Whether you've come for breakfast, lunch or dinner, my greengrocer roots (pardon the pun!) mean that fresh and seasonal food is at the heart of everything we do: we are still that same tiny cafe on the south coast, where it all began. .
